Movie trends in Banaglore -1st September 2012

 Followes:
September 7, 2012jdmaster  

Though this city may be filled the most brilliant IT minds, most of them still like to unwind on a weekend by going to watch a good movie. As per the enquiries received at Justdial Bangalore, Sudigadu is in the lead by 22% followed by Ek Tha Tiger at 21%, The Expendables 2 at 20%, Julayi at 20% and Mugamoodi at 17%.

Brief synopsis of the top 5 movies

Sudigadu: This movie pokes fun at all the antics of southern stars like Mahesh Babu, Pawan Kalyan and Balakrishna to Nagarjuna, Venkatesh etc. Shiva (Allari Naresh) has been a natural born hero and this is proved when he kills a villain’s son as a child. He gets separated from his parents as a baby but survives the harsh world. He grows up and falls in love with Priya (Monal Gajjar), however he needs to find his parents to tie the knot with her. There are other sub plots in which Shiva shows his hilarious superhuman abilities. The film is directed by Bhimaneni Srinivasa Rao and stars Allari Naresh, Monal Gajjar, Brahmanandam, Sayaji Shinde, Jayaprakash Reddy, M S Narayana, Hema.

Ek Tha Tiger: Tiger (Salman Khan) is India’s best RAW (Research & Analysis Wing) agent who is sent all over world to take on spies and agents by his boss. On one particular mission to Ireland he falls in love with a girl named Zoya (Katrina Kaif) who ironically turns out to be an ISI agent. They go their separate ways but only to reunite at a peace summit in Istanbul. They elope and now the good as well as bad guys are after them. The movie stars Salman Khan, Katrina Kaif, Girish Karnad, Ranvir Shorey and is directed by Kabir Khan.

The Expendables 2: The men of steel have reunited for yet another task. This is an out and out action movie. Tanks, guns, explosions, fast bikes etc in the heart of the jungle. All the actors are great but special mention needs to be made for Sylvester Stallone and Jason Statham who play a major role in the movie, they are simply fantastic. This is a good treat for action movie lovers. The movie is directed by Simon West and stars Sylvester Stallone, Jason Statham, Jet Li, Chuck Norris, Jean-Claude Van Damme, Bruce Willis, Arnold Schwarzenegger and Liam Hemsworth.

Julayi: Bittu (Sonu Sood) is a notorious bank robber who robs Rs 1,500 crore in a daring bank heist, his accidently run Ravindra (Allu Arjun) gets him caught by police. The police now send Ravindra to Hyderabad to stay with ACP Sitaram’s (Rajendra Prasad) for protection. Here he falls in love with Madhu (Ileana) just when things seem alright Bittu comes back for revenge. The movie stars Allu Arjun, Ileana, Sonu Sood, Rajendra Prasad, Rao Ramesh, Kota Srinivasa Rao, Tanikella Bharani, Brahmanadam, MS Narayana and is directed by Trivikram Srinivas.

Mugamoodi: Anand (Jiiva) is called Bruce Lee by his friends because his one and only love is kung fu fighting. To impress Shakti (Pooja Hegde), he wears a mask and cape but wrongly gets accused of trying to murder her father, assistant commissioner Gaurav (Nasser). He is now forced to take on the persona of a superhero ‘Mugamoodi’ (The mask) to clear his name and avenge his friend who gets killed by the goons. The movie stars Jiiva, Narain, Pooja Hegde, Selvaah, Nasser, Girish Karnad and is directed by Mysskin.

Theatres where these movies are playing

Sudigadu: Pvr Cinemas Koramangala, Inox Cinemas Malleswaram, Cinepolis Theatre Bannerghatta Road, Innovative Multiplex Marathahalli and Cinemax Cinemas Bellandur.

Ek Tha Tiger: Gopalan Cinemas Rajarajeshwari Nagar, Cinepolis Theatre Bannerghatta Road, Fame Cinemas Ulsoor, Inox Cinemas Jayanagar and Fun Cinemas Cunningham Road.

The Expendables 2: Fame Cinemas Ulsoor, Fun Cinemas Cunningham Road, Gopalan Cinemas Bannerghatta Road, Inox Cinemas J P Nagar and Innovative Multiplex Marathahalli.

Julayi: Inox Cinemas J P Nagar, Innovative Multiplex Marathahalli, Fame Cinemas Whitefield Main Road, Cinemax Cinemas Mahadevapura and Bhumika Theatre Gandhi Nagar.

Mugamoodi: Gopalan Cinemas Rajarajeshwari Nagar, Cinepolis Theatre Bannerghatta Road, Fame Cinemas Ulsoor, Inox Cinemas Jayanagar and Fun Cinemas Cunningham Road.
